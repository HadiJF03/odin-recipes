Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A Grilled Cheese sandwich

a grilled cheese made with butter and cheese. look bellow for a list of ingredients and steps.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white beard
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  • preheat non stick skillet.
  • butter bread on one side then place buttered side on skillet.
  • add 1 slice of cheese on the bread.
  • butter other slice of bread and place on cheese with butter side up.
  • flip when lightly bornwed and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  • enjot your grilled cheese!
